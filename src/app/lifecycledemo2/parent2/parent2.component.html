    Lifecycle Hooks
  • Components and Directives have Lifecycle hooks
  • Lifecycle hooks available to both Components and Directives
        ngOnInit, ngOnDestroy, ngOnChanges, ngDoCheck
  • Lifecycle hooks only available to Components
        ngAfterContentInit, ngAfterContentChecked, ngAfterViewInit and ngAfterContentChecked
